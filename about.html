While most air purifier companies focus on premium pricing and complex features, we're building the opposite — technology that's accessible to every home. We believe every family deserves clean air, not as a luxury, but as a basic right that shouldn't break the bank.

Engineering-driven design

Development

Our team spent over a year researching filtration systems, airflow dynamics, and sensor technology before building the first prototype. Every decision — from the H13 HEPA filter grade to the gesture sensor placement — was validated through rigorous testing and real-world usage data.

At Hawaa, we're taking these engineering principles and applying them where they matter most: inside your home. We build air purifiers that don't just perform in lab conditions — they perform in yours.

What we care about

Company values

Honesty over hype

We publish lab reports, not marketing claims. Every number on our website is backed by independent testing. If we can't prove it, we don't say it.

Accessible by default

Clean air shouldn't be a premium lifestyle product. We design for the family buying their first purifier, not the one upgrading their third.

Sweat the details

From the click of a gesture sensor to the seal on a filter cartridge, we obsess over the small things. The experience is in the details no one notices until they're missing.

Build it here

Designed, engineered, and manufactured in India. Not because it's a marketing badge, but because we believe in building where we breathe.

No tools. No manual marathon. This is the entire process, start to finish.

Filter Care

Keep your filtration
performing at its best

Three layers do the work. A simple care rhythm keeps them at their best.

Every time

Switch off before handling

Always turn the purifier off before removing, cleaning, or replacing the filter.

Every month

Vacuum the outer pre-filter

Use a soft brush or vacuum gently to remove visible dust buildup.

Every 4–5 months

Replace when reminded

Hawaa will notify you when it's time to replace the filter after regular use.

Never

Wash the filter

The filter is not washable. Water can damage the HEPA and carbon layers.

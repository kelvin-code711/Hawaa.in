01 — Placement

Give it room
to breathe.

Your Hawaa purifies best when air can move freely in and out of the unit. Place it where nothing crowds or corners it.

Keep at least 3.5 feet of clearance on all sides — away from walls, corners, and furniture that might restrict airflow.

A flat, stable surface at least one foot above the floor gives Hawaa the best reach across your entire room.

Tap to replay  ·  Isometric top-down view

Near the centre

Central placement lets Hawaa reach every corner of the room with equal efficiency.

Away from sunlight

Direct sun heats the sensors. Keep Hawaa in a shaded spot for accurate air readings.

Elevated surface

Place at least 1 foot off the floor — a shelf or side table is ideal — for optimal circulation.

Open air path

Nothing above or below the unit. Hawaa pulls air from the base and expels clean air from the top.

02 — Power On

One connection.
A breath of difference.

Plug in using the included premium cable and press the soft-touch power ring. A gentle chime and a warm glow confirm Hawaa is alive.

  1. 01

    Locate the power port

    Found at the base of the unit, on the rear-facing panel — a recessed, flush port.

  2. 02

    Connect the cable

    Use only the included braided cable for clean, stable power delivery.

  3. 03

    Press the power ring

    One press wakes Hawaa. The top ring illuminates softly and the welcome chime plays.

You’re almost there

Phase 1 complete.

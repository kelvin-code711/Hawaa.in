Smart Control

Connect Hawaa to Google Home

Control your purifier from anywhere using the Google Home app.

  1. Turn on the purifier and tap the Wi-Fi icon on the control panel.

  2. Open your phone's Wi-Fi list and select hawaa-setup.

  3. A setup page will open automatically. Enter your home Wi-Fi SSID and password.

  4. Open the Google Home app → Add device → Search Hawaa → enter the device code to complete setup.
